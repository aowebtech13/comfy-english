    Hello, I'm Comfort

    Hi, I'm Comfort — and yes, learning English can feel comfy!

    Imagine a class where comfort, confidence, and fun come first. That’s exactly what we offer at Comfy English. Whether you're learning from scratch or aiming for fluency, we create a warm, relaxed space where you can truly thrive.

    I’m a certified English tutor with 7+ years of experience, a TEFL certification, and a Master's in English. My team and I have helped over 1,000 students from countries like Russia, Turkey, and the US succeed in English and ace their exams.

    I’m also a UNESCO-MGIEP certified digital teacher, which means I use technology to make online learning smooth, interactive, and enjoyable.

    We’re here to make your English journey simple, personalized, and totally stress-free.
