    Experienced and Certified ESL Tutor

    I am a seasoned educator with over five years of experience in teaching English language and literature. Holding a B.A.Ed in English Education and a TEFL certification with distinction, I have successfully guided students through various curricula, including Nigerian and British standards, and prepared them for exams like WAEC, NECO, JAMB, GCSE, IELTS, SAT, and GRE.

    With expertise in both physical and online teaching methods, I have worked with renowned institutions such as Trailblazers Educational Consult, Lifeline Educational Consult, Lifeline Educational Institute, and Edswot. I am well-versed in utilizing e-learning platforms like Google Classroom, WhatsApp, Merithub, and Zoom to create engaging and accessible learning experiences.

    My teaching approach is hybrid, balancing teacher- and student-centred learning, to cater to the unique needs of each student. My students have achieved remarkable success, with many scoring 7.5 and above in their IELTS exams and over 80% in their university entry exams. I take pride in helping students overcome their academic challenges and reach their full potential.

    I am passionate about breaking down barriers to education, believing that learning should be unrestricted by time and space. I look forward to leveraging his teaching expertise and digital skills to make a meaningful impact in his next role.
