    • Vocabulary: Family, school, home ,weather , hobbies.
    • Bonus:Flashcards, worksheets, interactive activities

    A2- Programme
    (5.0)

    Elementary

    Speak with confidence! Build longer sentences and handle everyday conversations.

    • Grammer : Intensifiers, polite requests, first conditional

    • Vocabulary: Food , fashion , school , city life
    • Bonus:Worksheet , interactive activities , role plays
    • With regular lessons, you'll advance to A2 within a few months and expand your fluency!

    • Vocabulary: Media , Health , Personal growth.
